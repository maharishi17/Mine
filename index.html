Hi Guys Na Yungal Maharishi

Short Story Of Me: --- I am Maharishi, born on November 17, 2003. From a young age, I developed a passion for football, inspired by watching the game on TV. I became a devoted fan of Ronaldo, who has always been my stress buster and source of motivation. Now, at 21, I remain an ardent supporter of Ronaldo. However, my focus has shifted towards building a career as a full stack developer, a field that I find incredibly interesting and fulfilling.

My serenity boosters

Clouds

I love clouds because every time I gaze at them, I feel an immense sense of happiness and relief. Their gentle drift across the sky brings a soothing calmness to my mind. Whether they are fluffy white cumulus clouds floating lazily on a summer day or the dramatic, dark storm clouds signaling rain, their presence always uplifts my spirit. Watching clouds allows me to escape the stresses of everyday life, offering a moment of pure tranquility and joy.

Greeny

I love green nature because whenever I am surrounded by it, I feel an overwhelming sense of happiness and relief. The vibrant greenery of trees, grass, and plants has a calming effect on my mind, washing away the stresses of daily life. The sight of lush landscapes, whether it's a dense forest, a sprawling meadow, or a serene garden, fills me with peace and rejuvenation. The fresh air, the sound of rustling leaves, and the vibrant hues of green all work together to uplift my spirit.

Machis

My friends hold a special place in my heart, and whenever I am with Saravana, Prasana, Barani, Vijay, and Suriya, I experience true happiness. Our friendship, which has flourished for over 10 years, is a testament to the deep bond we share. These friends have become my chosen family, and their companionship brings immense joy and comfort into my life. Whether we are laughing together, sharing our dreams, or supporting each other through tough times, every moment spent with them is precious.
